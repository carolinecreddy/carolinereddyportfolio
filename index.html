About me

Curious about the environment, motivated by people, and committed to meaningful change.

I am drawn to Environmental Studies because the world is changing quickly, and many of those changes create serious challenges for people, communities, and ecosystems. I want to understand issues like climate change, environmental health, food systems, and public well-being deeply enough to contribute to solutions that are practical, thoughtful, and lasting.

My academic path at Tulane has given me a strong foundation in environmental thinking, while my hands-on experiences have shown me how sustainability connects to everyday community life. Whether I am working in a garden, supporting a local organization, or helping customers in a fast-paced setting, I value work that is useful, organized, and rooted in care for others.
